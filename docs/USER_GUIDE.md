# PAO: Master User Guide

Welcome to PAO! This guide will help you master the professional automation tools provided by the platform.

## 1. Professional Networking (Graph)

Manage your professional graph like a pro:
- **People Tab**: View and add contacts. Click "Enrich" to let AI auto-complete their profiles from public data.
- **Organizations Tab**: Track companies and agencies. PAO supports complex hierarchies (Parent/Child).
- **Networking**: Connect people to organizations and each other. Create categories like "Recruiter", "Colleague", and "Reports To".

## 2. Job Tracker (Leads)

Move beyond simple spreadsheets with the **Lead Intelligence System**:
- **Targeting**: Add a "Job Profile" (e.g., VP of AI) to define your high-level goals.
- **Intake**: Add jobs by pasting URLs. PAO will automatically attempt to scrape the requirements and extract key technical stacks using AI.
- **Phases**: Track your progress from "Target" to "Applied", "Interviewing", and "Offered".

## 3. Resume Architect (The Suite)

Generate world-class, tailored resumes in seconds:
- **Snippets**: Create a library of your career achievements. Each snippet should highlight different facets (e.g., ROI, Technical Stack, Leadership).
- **Generator**: Select a Job Lead, pick your best snippets, and click "AI Generate". PAO will synthesize a cohesive Markdown resume.
- **Exporting**: Use the built-in PDF/Word conversion service to finalize your document for submission.

## 4. Elevator Pitch Mastery (Practice Mode)

When you land the interview, be ready to shine:
- **AI Generator**: Enter 3-4 bullets of your biggest wins and your target role. PAO will craft a high-impact, 30-second introduction.
- **The Teleprompter**: Launch "Practice Mode" for a full-screen, distraction-free rehearsal experience.
  - **Adjust Speed**: Speed up or slow down the scrolling to match your natural speaking pace.
  - **Visual Pacing**: Read only the text within the highlighted "Active Area" to ensure perfect timing.

## 5. System Health & Performance

- **Dashboard**: Monitor stock prices of target companies or track your latest activity logs.
- **AI Status**: Check the `task_logs` if an operation seems to take longer than usual. PAO may be failing over to its local Ollama engine to ensure your work stays uninterrupted.
